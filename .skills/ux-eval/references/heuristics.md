# UX Review Heuristics

Use these dimensions when reviewing the rendered experience.

## 1. UI Polish
- spacing consistency
- alignment precision
- typography hierarchy
- contrast and readability
- visual grouping
- CTA emphasis
- iconography clarity
- density and clutter

## 2. UX Flow
- clarity of next step
- natural sequencing
- cognitive load
- unnecessary choices
- dead ends or loops
- interruption handling
- predictability of outcomes
- progress visibility

## 3. Interaction Quality
- obvious click targets
- hover, focus, active feedback
- control affordance
- validation timing and clarity
- empty state usefulness
- loading state clarity
- success and error recovery

## 4. Trust And Comprehension
- does the user understand what is happening
- does the UI feel credible and deliberate
- are labels concrete and expectation-setting
- are risks and consequences explained at the right moment

## 5. Responsive Behavior
- hierarchy retention at smaller widths
- tap target sizing
- overflow and truncation
- spacing collapse
- sticky footer or CTA behavior

## Severity Guidance
- High: likely blocks completion, creates serious confusion, or undermines trust
- Medium: meaningfully increases friction or hesitation
- Low: polish issue or mild friction that still weakens overall quality
