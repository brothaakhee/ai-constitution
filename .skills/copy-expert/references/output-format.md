# Copy Expert Output Format

Return findings first.

## Finding Template
1. Severity: short title
Location: screen, flow, or artifact
Current issue: what is weak or unclear in the messaging
Likely user reaction: what the user may think, feel, or infer
Why it matters: why this hurts clarity, trust, positioning, or conversion
Suggestion: high-level improvement direction
Sample rewrite: optional example only

## Closing Sections
- Audience or tone assumptions
- Strengths
- Open questions
- Unreviewed areas

Keep the report concise, concrete, and suggestion-oriented.
